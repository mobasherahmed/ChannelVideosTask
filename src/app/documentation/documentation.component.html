Overview

This App displaying list of videos from TEDx Channel , First page is the home page that displaying the list in a table view including (Title – Thumbnail – Date - View Details button) , Search bar above table for search by video title and sorting each column descend or asecend also table pagination for multi pages, Second page is the video details showing all video details including (# of likes - # of dislikes - # of viewers - # of comments - Title - Thumbnail – Date - Duration of video - Description - rate) With the option to add video to favourite list and also rating it .

Scenario

After App run it will redirect to home page automatically , calling api that return list of videos showing on a table view , then all videos cached and stored in a local stoarge variable called Videos to easily get result on offline mode , In Home Page you can search , sort and so on go to video details by clicking on Video Details button . In Video Deatils Page all video details are shown and you can add video to your favourite list by clicking on Add to favourite button , After Clicking on this button video id will be stored in a new local stoarge array called Fav to determine that this video is already in your favourites or not on the next time , also you can Rate the video , After rating the video it will be stored in new array in local stoarge called Rating with the video id to get the old rate on the next time .
